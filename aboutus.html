About Us

Founded in 2001, MediCare started as a small family-owned healthcare store in Kemanggisan, Jakarta. As more and more people get to know about MediCare, it is natural for us to expand our stores to other cities and provinces. Now, we have more than 100 stores spread out in almost every major cities in Indoensia, including but no limited to Aceh, Medan, Palembang, Jakarta, Surabaya, Pontianak, Balikpapan and Makassar. We also offers a wider variety for producrs from healthcare, cosmetics, medicines, and many more. We have Commiment to stay true to our humble origin and serving our customers with best customer services and high quality products.
